Education

My academic history and details, like relevant courses and honors.

Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) | Bachelor of Science in Control and Instrumentation | 2016-09 - 2021-04

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Control and Instrumentation from JKUAT. Below are some of the relevant courses I undertook during my studies:

Relevant Control and Instrumentation Courses

  • Control Systems Engineering (CSE 3101) – Analysis, modeling, and design of control systems for industrial automation and robotics.
  • Process Control (PCE 4101) – Techniques for regulating temperature, pressure, and flow in manufacturing plants and refineries.
  • Industrial Automation (IAU 4201) – PLC programming, SCADA systems, and HMI design for smart factories and industrial processes.
  • Instrumentation and Measurement (IME 3201) – Selection, calibration, and integration of sensors and transducers in industrial environments.
  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers (MCE 3202) – Embedded systems programming for IoT, robotics, and automation projects.
  • Digital Signal Processing (DSP 4101) – Signal filtering, Fourier transforms, and audio/video signal applications in control and instrumentation.
  • Robotics and Automation (RBA 4201) – Autonomous system control, kinematics, and sensor integration for robotic applications.
  • Electromagnetic Fields and Waves (EMF 3102) – Essential for working with RF sensors, wireless control, and electromagnetic interference (EMI) management.

Relevant Electrical and Electronics Engineering Courses

  • Electric Circuits and Systems (ECS 2101) – Design and analysis of DC/AC circuits, essential for electrical engineers and electronics design.
  • Power Electronics (PEL 4101) – Design of inverters, rectifiers, and motor drive circuits for renewable energy and electric vehicle applications.
  • Electrical Machines (EME 3201) – Operation and maintenance of transformers, generators, and electric motors in industrial plants.
  • Electricity and Magnetism (ELM 3101) – Fundamental electromagnetism principles applied in power transmission and electromagnetic devices.
  • Energy Conversion and Renewable Energy (ERE 4202) – Practical applications of solar, wind, and hydroelectric power generation.
  • High Voltage Engineering (HVE 4102) – Design, insulation, and protection techniques for power transmission and distribution systems.
  • Embedded Systems Design (ESD 3202) – Development of firmware and hardware interfaces for industrial and IoT applications.

Computer and Software Courses

  • Programming for Engineers (CSE 1101) – Python, C, and MATLAB for developing automation, control, and AI-based systems.
  • Computer Networking (CNE 3103) – Network protocols, IoT device communication, and cybersecurity principles for industrial networks.
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIL 4201) – Application of AI in automation, predictive maintenance, and industrial robotics.
  • Data Acquisition and Processing (DAP 4103) – Collection and analysis of real-time industrial data for process optimization.

Supporting Classwork

  • Engineering Mathematics (MAT 3101) – Advanced calculus, differential equations, and linear algebra for modeling control systems.
  • Statistics and Probability (STA 2100) – Data analysis, statistical modeling, and reliability testing in engineering applications.
  • Technical Communication (TEC 1101) – Writing reports, presenting findings, and communicating with clients and stakeholders.
  • Project Management and Engineering Ethics (PME 4203) – Managing engineering projects, budgeting, and professional responsibility.
  • Entrepreneurship for Engineers (ENT 4104) – Business development, contract negotiation, and consulting for freelancers.
